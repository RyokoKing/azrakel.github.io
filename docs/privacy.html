Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 21, 2025

1. Introduction

Azrakel is a Discord bot developed and maintained by Team BarkByte. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our services. By using Azrakel, you agree to the practices described in this document.

2. Data We Collect

  • Discord User Information: Usernames, IDs, avatars (as provided by Discord APIs)
  • Server Information: Server IDs, names, and configuration data for moderation settings
  • Command Usage Logs: Certain moderation commands (e.g. /warn) may be logged locally to a secure storage location for moderation reference only
  • No Messages or Content: Azrakel does not store message content or conversations

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve bot features and services
  • To maintain moderation logs (e.g. warnings) where applicable
  • To diagnose and fix bugs or performance issues

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or rent your data to any third party. Any data collected is used solely for providing the intended functionality of Azrakel. We comply with Discord’s Developer Terms and US online privacy regulations.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on the bot owner's machine or encrypted server with appropriate security measures. Access is limited to Team BarkByte. Regular reviews are performed to ensure outdated or unnecessary data is deleted.

6. User Rights

If you are a server administrator or user and would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please contact us at teambarkbyte@gmail.com. We will comply in accordance with Discord policies and applicable laws.

7. Children's Privacy

Azrakel is intended for use on Discord by users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 13 in accordance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of Azrakel signifies your acceptance of these changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle data, you can contact us at: teambarkbyte@gmail.com.

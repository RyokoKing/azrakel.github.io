Bot Commands

Azrakel offers a suite of useful tools for moderation, engagement, and fun.

Moderation

  • /kick <user> [reason] – Remove a user from the server.
  • /ban <user> [reason] – Ban a user with an optional reason.
  • /mute <user> [reason] – Assigns a muted role to the user.
  • /warn <user> <reason> – Issues a warning to the user.
  • /view_warnings <user> – View warnings issued to a user.
  • /clear_warnings <user> – Clear a user's warnings.
  • /report <user> <reason> – Report a user to the moderation team.

Fun & Interaction

  • /about – Learn more about the Azrakel bot.
  • /hug [user] – Send a comforting virtual hug.
  • /joke – Hear a fun and lighthearted joke.
  • /riddle – Get a random riddle to challenge your mind.
  • /howl – Unleash a haunting howl into the digital forest.
  • /trail – Receive a nature-inspired tip or folklore fact.
  • /fortune – Receive a creepy digital fortune.
  • /quote – Get a cryptic quote from beyond.
  • /whisper – Hear a spooky message just for you.
  • /omen – Receive a mysterious omen.
  • /vision – Be shown a disturbing vision.
  • /curse <user> – Place a playful packet loss curse.
  • /uncurse <user> – Lift said packet loss curse.

Utility & Misc

  • /ping – Check if Azrakel is alive and responsive.
  • /uptime – See how long Azrakel has been online.
  • /serverinfo – View basic information about the current server.
  • /userinfo [user] – View account details for a member.
  • /roll [sides] – Roll a dice with custom sides (default is 6).
  • /bug <description> – Submit a bug report for the developers.
  • /suggest <idea> – Submit a feature suggestion for Azrakel.
  • /status – Get the bot’s current operational status.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Azrakel?

Azrakel is a Discord bot developed by Team BarkByte. It offers moderation tools, entertainment commands, and a distinctive dark aesthetic designed for community engagement and control.

What features are available?

Azrakel provides commands for moderation (e.g., kick, ban, mute, warn), utility, and fun interactions like riddles and jokes. A complete list is available on the Commands page.

Is Azrakel safe to use?

Yes. Azrakel does not read or store message content. It complies with Discord’s API Terms of Service and only collects data necessary to perform its functions. Please review the Privacy Policy for full details.

What data does Azrakel collect?

Azrakel may store user IDs, usernames, server IDs, and logs related to moderation commands (such as warnings). No message content is recorded. All data is stored securely and used only for server moderation.

Where is data stored?

Data is stored locally or on secure, encrypted servers managed by the bot's developer. Team BarkByte performs regular checks to ensure outdated data is deleted in compliance with internal retention policies.

Can I request my data be deleted?

Yes. If you are a server administrator or user, you may request deletion of your moderation data by contacting teambarkbyte@gmail.com. We will respond in compliance with Discord's policies and applicable laws.

Is Azrakel compliant with privacy laws?

Yes. Azrakel is compliant with Discord's Developer Terms of Service, the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and general online privacy standards. We take data security and ethical development seriously.

Can I suggest new features?

Suggestions are welcome. You may contact Team BarkByte via email or submit ideas through our future support server. Feature requests are reviewed regularly and may be added based on community interest.

What should I do if the bot stops working?

First, ensure the bot has the correct permissions and is online. If problems persist, try reinviting the bot. If the issue continues, contact support at teambarkbyte@gmail.com.

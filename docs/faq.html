Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who made Azrakel?
A: Azrakel is developed and maintained by Team BarkByte.

Q: Does Azrakel store any user data?
A: Only moderation logs (warnings/reports) are stored, and only on the host machine. Nothing is sent to third parties.

Q: Can I invite Azrakel to my server?
A: Currently, Azrakel is private. Stay tuned for public release updates.
